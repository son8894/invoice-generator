### Full Description

**Automate Your Invoice Generation**

Invoice Generator creates professional PDF invoices automatically for every order placed in your store. Save time, reduce manual work, and provide professional documentation to your customers.

**Key Features:**

✅ **Automatic Invoice Generation** - Invoices are created automatically when orders are placed (requires app approval)

✅ **Multi-Language Support** - Generate invoices in English, Korean (한국어), or Japanese (日本語)

✅ **Customizable Company Information** - Add your logo, address, tax ID, and contact details

✅ **Professional PDF Output** - Clean, professional invoice templates

✅ **Easy Download** - Download any invoice with one click

✅ **Manual Invoice Creation** - Generate invoices for existing orders anytime

✅ **Order History** - View all generated invoices in one place

✅ **No Email Sending** - Simple PDF download system (email integration coming soon)

**Perfect For:**

- Stores that need professional invoices for B2B customers
- International merchants serving Asian markets (Korea, Japan)
- Stores with Plus plans looking for invoice automation
- Businesses requiring tax documentation

**How It Works:**

1. **Configure Your Settings** - Add your company information and choose your invoice language
2. **Orders → Invoices** - Invoices are automatically generated for new orders
3. **Download Anytime** - Access and download any invoice from your dashboard
4. **Customize** - Update company information anytime

**Language Support:**

- **English** - Full template support
- **한국어 (Korean)** - Native Korean invoice templates
- **日本語 (Japanese)** - Native Japanese invoice templates

## Testing Instructions for Shopify Review Team

1. **Installation**: Install the app on your dev store
2. **Configuration**: 
   - Go to Settings
   - Enter company information (any test data)
   - Select invoice language (English, Korean, or Japanese)
   - Click Save
3. **Create Invoice**:
   - Go to Dashboard
   - Enter an existing order ID (e.g., create a test order first)
   - Click "Generate Invoice"
4. **Download PDF**:
   - Click "Download PDF" button on any invoice
   - Verify PDF opens and displays correctly
5. **Test Languages**:
   - Go to Settings
   - Change language to Korean, save
   - Generate new invoice, download, verify Korean template
   - Repeat for Japanese
