# Privacy Policy for Invoice Generator

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## Introduction

Invoice Generator ("we", "our", or "us") is a Shopify app that helps merchants automatically generate PDF invoices for their orders. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Shopify Store Information
- Store domain and name
- Store owner contact information
- Access tokens for API authentication

### 2. Order Data
When generating invoices, we access and process:
- Order numbers and IDs
- Customer names and email addresses
- Order totals and currency
- Order creation dates

### 3. Company Settings
Information you provide to customize invoices:
- Company name
- Business address
- Tax ID / Business number
- Contact email and phone
- Invoice language preference

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Generate Invoices**: Create PDF invoices for your orders
2. **Provide Service**: Deliver core app functionality
3. **Store Settings**: Save your company information and preferences
4. **Improve Experience**: Enhance app features and usability

## Data Storage

- **Database**: We use Neon PostgreSQL to store invoice records and settings
- **Location**: Data is stored in Singapore (ap-southeast-1) servers
- **Retention**: Invoice data is retained while you use the app
- **Deletion**: When you uninstall the app, all your data is deleted within 30 days

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Access customer data beyond what's necessary for invoice generation

We **MAY** share data with:
- **Shopify**: As required by their API and app platform
- **Hosting Provider (Render.com)**: For app infrastructure
- **Database Provider (Neon)**: For data storage

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure database connections
- Regular security updates
- Access controls and authentication
